# CRM Commercial Findings

These findings come from the derived summaries in `d3/data/`.

They are the commercial anchor for Deliverable 3.

## Cross-Country Topline

- France is by far the largest target market in the CRM base:
  - `4,140` rows
  - about `€6.80bn` AUM
- Switzerland and Spain are the next most material non-France markets in the target perimeter:
  - Switzerland: about `€1.61bn`
  - Spain: about `€1.54bn`
- Italy is smaller in row count but still meaningful by AUM:
  - about `€1.03bn`
- Germany is the smallest of the five in this extract:
  - about `€315m`

## Country Readouts

## France

- largest and broadest market in the target set
- strongest channel by AUM:
  - `Insurance Company / Unit Link`
- other important channels:
  - `Bank / EdR Private Banking`
  - `AM company / Fund of Funds`
  - `Bank / Private Banking`
- leading funds by AUM:
  - `EdR Fund Big Data`
  - `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
  - `EdR SICAV - Tricolore Convictions`
  - `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`
  - `EdR SICAV - Millesima 2030`

Strategic implication:
France should not be reduced to one product story. It can support a domestic/euro equity angle, a flagship thematic growth angle, and a scalable fixed-income or packaged-income angle.

## Germany

- smallest target market in both rows and AUM
- distribution is unusually concentrated
- largest channel by AUM:
  - `AM company / Fund of Funds`
- largest channel by row count:
  - `Bank / Trading Platform`
- standout fund by AUM:
  - `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- next tier:
  - `EdR Fund Big Data`
  - `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`

Strategic implication:
Germany should likely be treated as a selective execution market. The final recommendation set should be short, liquid, and easy to defend on platforms and screens.

## Switzerland

- very strong private-banking profile
- dominant channel by AUM and row count:
  - `Bank / EdR Private Banking`
- second core channel:
  - `Bank / Private Banking`
- leading funds by AUM:
  - `EDR SICAV - Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
  - `EdR Fund Big Data`
  - `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
  - `EdR Fund Emerging Credit`
  - `EdR Fund Investment Grade Credit`

Strategic implication:
Switzerland is the most natural market for sophisticated yield, credit, and curated thematic recommendations. This page should feel wealth-management specific.

## Italy

- moderate scale but high concentration in a few product/channel clusters
- leading channels by AUM:
  - `Insurance Company / Own Account`
  - `AM company / Fund of Funds`
  - `Bank / EdR Private Banking`
- leading funds by AUM:
  - `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
  - `EdR Fund Big Data`
  - `EDR SICAV - Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
  - `EdR SICAV - Short Duration Credit`

Strategic implication:
Italy looks like a strong market for income, carry, and distributor-friendly credit ideas, with one growth/thematic sleeve to avoid an overly defensive page.

## Spain

- strong base in private banking and fund-of-funds distribution
- leading channels by AUM:
  - `Bank / Private Banking`
  - `AM company / Fund of Funds`
  - `Bank / Retail`
  - `Bank / EdR Private Banking`
- leading funds by AUM:
  - `EdR Fund Big Data`
  - `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
  - `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`
  - `EdR SICAV - Millesima 2030`
  - `EdR SICAV - Millesima Select 2028`

Strategic implication:
Spain is one of the clearest cases for a barbell:

- one flagship thematic winner
- one core income / carry idea
- one packaged maturity product for distributor-friendly conversations

## Cross-Country Strategic Signals

### Big Data Is Already Commercially Proven

`EdR Fund Big Data` is a serious installed product in France, Switzerland, and Spain, and still visible in Germany and Italy.

Implication:
We should assume this fund deserves explicit review in every country, but we should not force it into all five final recommendations if a local sales story is weaker.

### Financial Bonds Is A Structural D3 Candidate

`EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds` is one of the most powerful recurring products across the five-country perimeter and dominates Germany and Italy by AUM.

Implication:
If the 2026 macro story still supports carry, spread products, and selective credit over pure duration, this fund could become one of the backbone D3 recommendations.

### Maturity Solutions Matter More In Distributor Markets

The `Millesima` range is especially relevant in France and Spain.

Implication:
Packaged income / defined-maturity products should not be treated as secondary. They may be some of the most sales-ready recommendations in the final deliverable.

### Switzerland Deserves A Different Language

Switzerland is not just another European market with good AUM. Its private-banking concentration means product sophistication and portfolio-construction logic should be more explicit there.

### Germany Needs Restraint

Germany is the easiest country to over-engineer. The market base in this CRM extract is relatively small and platform-heavy.

Implication:
The final German page should be tighter than the others and avoid product sprawl.
