# Preliminary Country Theses

This note is the bridge between Phase 1 intelligence and Deliverable 3 commercialization.

These are not final recommendations yet. They are the first country-specific hypotheses strong enough to test.

## France

### Local Macro Lens

Likely emphasis:

- Europe recovery and fiscal support
- selective equity risk, not blind beta
- strategic autonomy / domestic champions
- room for both flagship thematic growth and scalable income products

Why:

- the French and French-adjacent manager set is rich in Europe-recovery logic
- EdRAM itself is more constructive on Europe than on crowded U.S. mega-cap AI
- the commercial base is deep enough to support multiple recommendation types

### Commercial Reality

- biggest target market by a wide margin
- important insurance / unit-link footprint
- strong installed base in:
  - `Big Data`
  - `Financial Bonds`
  - `Tricolore Convictions`
  - `Bond Allocation`

### Candidate D3 Pool

- `Tricolore Convictions`
- `EdR Fund Big Data`
- `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`
- `EdR SICAV - Millesima 2030`

### Working Hypothesis

France should probably be one of the few countries where we can justify a domestic/euro equity recommendation explicitly, rather than defaulting only to credit and carry.

## Germany

### Local Macro Lens

Likely emphasis:

- disciplined macro normalization
- support from German fiscal impulse and European rearmament / infrastructure
- selectivity over broad market enthusiasm
- products that are easy to justify on institutional and platform screens

### Commercial Reality

- smallest target market in the CRM base
- high concentration in platforms and a small number of products
- `Financial Bonds` dominates installed AUM

### Candidate D3 Pool

- `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- `EdR Fund Big Data`
- `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`
- `EdR SICAV - Euro Sustainable Equity`

### Working Hypothesis

Germany should likely be the most concentrated page in the deck:

- one flagship income idea
- one flagship thematic idea
- one simpler portfolio-allocation back-up

Anything more may look theoretical rather than executable.

## Switzerland

### Local Macro Lens

Likely emphasis:

- resilient growth, but with wealth-style portfolio construction
- credit carry and sophisticated income
- selective thematic growth rather than benchmark-chasing
- strong relevance of private-bank product suitability

### Commercial Reality

- one of the strongest target markets by AUM
- heavily private-banking concentrated
- strong traction in:
  - `Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
  - `Financial Bonds`
  - `Emerging Credit`
  - `Big Data`

### Candidate D3 Pool

- `EDR SICAV - Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
- `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- `EdR Fund Emerging Credit`
- `EdR Fund Big Data`
- `EdR Fund Investment Grade Credit`

### Working Hypothesis

Switzerland is the cleanest market for a recommendation set that looks curated and sophisticated, not mass-market:

- one premium carry product
- one broader credit idea
- one high-conviction thematic winner

## Italy

### Local Macro Lens

Likely emphasis:

- income, carry, and product packaging
- selective cyclical recovery rather than aggressive growth
- strong value in spread products over duration
- distributor sensitivity

### Commercial Reality

- relatively concentrated by both channels and products
- strongest AUM sits in:
  - `Financial Bonds`
  - `Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
  - `Big Data`
  - `Short Duration Credit`

### Candidate D3 Pool

- `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- `EDR SICAV - Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
- `EdR SICAV - Short Duration Credit`
- `EdR Fund Big Data`
- `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`

### Working Hypothesis

Italy should probably be anchored in fixed-income carry and yield solutions, with only one selective growth product kept in the final top three.

## Spain

### Local Macro Lens

Likely emphasis:

- constructive but pragmatic risk-taking
- one clear thematic winner
- one or two distributor-friendly income solutions
- suitability for private banking and fund-of-funds conversations

### Commercial Reality

- strong target market by AUM
- private banking and fund-of-funds matter a lot
- strong installed base in:
  - `Big Data`
  - `Financial Bonds`
  - `Bond Allocation`
  - `Millesima 2030`
  - `Millesima Select 2028`

### Candidate D3 Pool

- `EdR Fund Big Data`
- `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`
- `EdR SICAV - Millesima 2030`
- `EdR SICAV - Millesima Select 2028`
- `EdR SICAV - Short Duration Credit`

### Working Hypothesis

Spain is one of the strongest cases for a three-part commercial architecture:

- flagship thematic growth
- core carry / bond solution
- packaged maturity product for distribution efficiency

## Strategic Rule For The Final Deck

If the same three funds appear in every country, the deliverable will look lazy.

A good final outcome should create visible differentiation such as:

- France = domestic / euro equity plus flagship thematic plus broad fixed-income scale
- Germany = tighter, platform-ready shortlist
- Switzerland = sophisticated credit and PB framing
- Italy = carry-first distributor logic
- Spain = thematic plus packaged-income barbell
