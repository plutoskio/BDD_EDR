# Draft Fund Taxonomy

This taxonomy is a working layer for Deliverable 3.

It is based on the fund names visible in the CRM extract and should be validated later against any fuller EdRAM product documentation if needed.

## 1. Thematic Growth / Innovation

- `EdR Fund Big Data`
- `EdR SICAV - Tech for Tomorrow`
- `EdR Fund Healthcare`
- `EdR Fund China`
- `EdR India`

Working use:

- AI adoption
- structural growth
- selective non-benchmark thematic exposure

## 2. Europe / Domestic Equity / Recovery

- `EdR SICAV - Tricolore Convictions`
- `EdR SICAV - Euro Sustainable Equity`
- `EdR SICAV - European Catalysts`
- `EdR SICAV - European Smaller Companies`
- `EdR Fund Income Europe`
- `EdR SICAV - Equity Euro Solve`

Working use:

- Europe recovery
- domestic demand and fiscal support
- catch-up trades versus crowded U.S. exposure

## 3. Core Allocation / Flexible Base

- `EdR Fund Bond Allocation`

Working use:

- balanced and simpler distributor conversations
- easier bridge product when the macro view is constructive but not aggressive

## 4. Credit Carry / Income

- `EdR SICAV - Financial Bonds`
- `EDR SICAV - Corporate Hybrid Bonds`
- `EdR SICAV - Short Duration Credit`
- `EdR Fund Investment Grade Credit`
- `EdR SICAV - Euro Sustainable Credit`
- `EdR Fund Euro High Yield`
- `EdR Fund Emerging Credit`
- `EdR Fund Emerging Sovereign`
- `EdR Fund EM Climate Bonds`

Working use:

- carry over duration
- spread-product preference
- selective income and resilience

## 5. Packaged Maturity Solutions

- `EdR SICAV - Millesima 2030`
- `EdR SICAV - Millesima Select 2028`
- `EdR SICAV - Millesima World 2028`

Working use:

- distributor-friendly sales pitches
- defined-horizon income conversations
- fund-of-funds and private-banking suitability in some markets

## 6. Style / Diversification Sleeves

- `EdR Fund US Value`
- `EdR Goldsphere`
- `EdR SICAV - Global Resilience`

Working use:

- diversification away from crowded growth
- resilience / hedge logic
- selective macro expression rather than core country recommendation

## D3 Rule

Each country should mix no more than three fund families in the final recommendation set.

If a country page includes too many product families, it will read like inventory rather than prioritization.
